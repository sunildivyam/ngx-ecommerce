Get started to recieve Orders

Checklist:

Before you make your product live and up for selling, Please check following:

  1. Title: Your product has a title that well identifies your product.
  2. Description: Your product has a description that well describes your product and its features.
  3. Product Images:Upload atleast 4 product images of good quality and of specified dimensions
  4. Sizes:Add all available sizes of your product, that you have in your inventory and want to sell.
  5. Price:Add price of each available size of your product that you want to sell excluding discounts and taxes.
  6. Qty:Add available quantities from your inventory for each available size. A zero qty means product is Out of Stock
  7. Offers:Add discounts in percentage of price if you want to offer to your customers for each size of product.
  8. Dispatch Period:Add minimum and maximum dispatch time, you need to after the order is placed. This is not delivery time. Delivery time additionally starts once your product is dispatched.

Hope you are good with your product. Go ahead, Happy Selling!
